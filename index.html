TOKENOMICS

RibbiFi's token distribution is designed to empower the community while creating sustainable growth of the ecosystem from day one.


  • Community-62.5%:
    The majority of tokens are reserved for the RibbiFi community. Whether through staking, farming, rewards, liquidity provisions, governance participation, and long term growth. The vesting ensures maximum benefits to the community.

  • Airdrop-25%:
    To reward early adopters from across the world. 20% of the supply is allocated for Initial Liquidity. Taking this into account ensures sufficient liquidity to make $RIBBI for distribution before a trade - 1.5% of total supply weekly in return to any participants a future that is built on algorithmic foundations and allows long term growth.

  • Team-12.5%:
    A fixed allocation for the RibbiFi core team. This portion accounts for the development, marketing, and operational costs. Team tokens will be vested in algo-incentives and allows long term growth.
